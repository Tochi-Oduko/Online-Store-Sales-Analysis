# Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## Business Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Amazon order transaction data to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales patterns. The insights are intended to support data-driven decisions in marketing, inventory planning, and seller performance evaluation.

---

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains 100,000 order-level records with the following key attributes:
- Order and customer information
- Product, category, and brand details
- Pricing, discounts, tax, and shipping costs
- Payment methods and order status
- Geographic location (City, State, Country)
- Seller identifiers

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables and PivotCharts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting

---

## Data Preparation
The following steps were performed:
- Removed unnecessary columns and blank fields
- Extracted time attributes (Month, Year) for trend analysis
- Created calculated field for product value
- Created calculated metrics:
  - Total Amount
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Total number of CustomerID, OrderID, SellerID

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### Sales Performance
- Revenue trend analysis over time
- Identification of peak sales periods

### Product & Category Analysis
- Top-performing products by revenue and quantity
- Category contribution to total sales

### Customer Analysis
- High-value customers based on total spend
- Purchase frequency patterns

### Geographic Analysis
- Sales distribution by country
- Identification of high-revenue regions

### Order & Payment Analysis
- Distribution of payment methods
- Order status breakdown (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned)

### Seller Performance
- Revenue contribution by seller
- Order completion rates

---

## Key Insights
- A small percentage of products contribute a significant portion of total revenue
- Certain categories consistently outperform others across all regions
- Sales peak during specific months, indicating seasonal demand
- Some regions generate high revenue but incur higher shipping costs
- Cash on Delivery has a higher cancellation rate compared to digital payments

---

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories and products
- Optimize shipping strategies in high-cost regions
- Promote digital payment methods to reduce cancellations
- Provide performance incentives to top-performing sellers
